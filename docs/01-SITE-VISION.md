# 01 — Site Vision

## Objective
Create a static website that feels more intentional, polished, cohesive, and useful to real customers. The site should be visually stronger than a generic template while still reading as credible, clear, and business-appropriate.

## What the site must do
- Explain what the business does quickly
- Establish credibility quickly
- Help a customer understand whether they are in the right place
- Route the customer to the correct next step
- Reduce confusion and cognitive load

## High-level goals
- Stronger visual identity
- Better UX flow and hierarchy
- Better section rhythm and pacing
- Better CTA placement and next-step clarity
- More tasteful motion and visual interest
- Cleaner, more relevant copy

## What "better" means here
- Easier to scan
- Easier to understand
- More visually cohesive
- More premium and deliberate
- Less templated
- More persuasive through clarity, not hype

## What the site must not become
- A vague brochure
- A startup landing page full of hype
- A generic science template
- A design playground that hides the core message
- A copy-heavy wall of text
